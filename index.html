This directory compiles reliable resources for people living with an ostomy and those supporting them. Use the navigation bar to jump between categories or the search box to filter by keyword. All external links open in a new tab so you can easily return to this list.

Product Selection Tools

Hollister – CeraPlus™ Product Selector

Answer seven questions about your stoma type, size, abdomen profile, skin health and lifestyle to receive a tailored skin‑barrier and pouch recommendation. Free samples are available.

Visit Hollister

ConvaTec – me+® Ostomy Product Selector Tool

Select your ostomy type, choose a one‑ or two‑piece system, flange diameter and extras like filters or convexity. Receive compatible product options and request no‑cost samples or nurse support.

Visit ConvaTec

Coloplast – BodyCheck™ & Self‑Assessment Tools

Interactive tools that guide you through your body profile in just a few minutes. They recommend adhesives, pouches and accessories and include how‑to videos and troubleshooting advice.

Visit Coloplast

Byram Healthcare – Ostomy Product Selection Guide

Filter across multiple brands (Hollister, ConvaTec, Coloplast, Marlen and more) by pouch type, barrier shape, flange size and cut‑to‑fit versus pre‑cut. Includes custom cutting programs and participation in manufacturer support schemes.

Visit Byram

StomaBags.com – Advanced Shop Filters

An online store with granular filters for one‑ or two‑piece systems, drainable or closed pouches, convexity, exact flange or stoma opening diameter and capacity. Ideal for comparison‑shopping once you know your measurements.

Visit StomaBags

Search by ZIP code or state for certified wound, ostomy and continence nurses. The WOCN Society does not endorse individual providers but helps you locate specialists nearby.

UOAA – Find an Ostomy Nurse

Use the United Ostomy Associations of America (UOAA) directory to locate ostomy nurses across the United States. Verify credentials and choose a provider who specialises in ostomy care.

Find an Ostomy Nurse

UOAA – Outpatient Ostomy Services Locator

A searchable directory of outpatient clinics offering ostomy support, including pouching instruction, stoma site marking and follow‑up care.

Locate Services

UOAA – Virtual Ostomy Clinic

Certified WOC nurses provide telehealth visits to assess your ostomy, select supplies, manage skin issues and answer questions via video, text or email. Includes follow‑ups and personalised education.

A complimentary programme providing product samples, personalised onboarding and ongoing support to help you succeed with your Hollister pouching system. Includes access to nurse coaching and educational resources.

Byram offers educational support through a Clinical CareLine and an Ostomy CareLine staffed by WOC nurses who can answer clinical questions after surgery. Byram also participates in manufacturer support programs such as Coloplast Care, ConvaTec me+ and Hollister Secure Start.
